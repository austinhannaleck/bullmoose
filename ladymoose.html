Product Guide

The beard world isn't so cut and dry. Whether you want the full suite of Signature, or are just looking to try something new, this handy guide below can help light the way.

Oil

Beard oil is the foundation for any good beard care set. It is used for quick conditioning as well as adding an extra amount of sheen to your beard. If you are unsure about what to get oil is always a safe bet. Best used on beards under a half an inch and on beards over 2 inches long.

Balm

Beard balm is the next step up from oil. This acts a long term conditioner because of the beeswax and shea butter the product takes more time to make its way into the hairs. Although this doesn't add the amount of sheen oil does it makes up for it with all day conditioning. This is ideal for the medium sized beards because in addition to the conditioning it also helps with taming the wildest of manes.

Wash

This is the best product to round out your beard care arsenal. Although it isn't used nearly as much as oil or balm its still a key player because it adds more conditioning in the shower when, normally, the beard is being stripped of its natural oils. Any beard length can use this product, but the longer the better, but that's the case with any beards.

Shave Butter

For the men who can not grow a luscious mane or who aren't allowed to this product is made just for you. Crafted with the one goal of making shaving easy and bearable this product is made with specific oils that target the skin and softens it as you shave, so it reduces the irritation caused by shaving and negates the need for shaving cream as well as after shave.
